Strengths


Patience

I'm a really collected, intelligent person, and I know the benefits of patience.
There will be difficulties, therefore patience is necessary.
There will be individuals that annoy you and circumstances that aggravate you.
If you're patient, you retain your focus on the task at hand. Instead of complaining, find a solution.


Ambitious

I'm ambitious, and in fact, I consider it to be one of my best qualities.
In some ways, having ambition raises my bar for success.

Versatile

My versatility is without a doubt one of my strongest traits.
Simply said, my ability to be versatile helps me to maximize my productivity.
I can take use of diverse skill sets by going beyond established job descriptions.

The Summary Of The Book Twelve Pillars(Chapter 4-7)

Several things were evident to me after reading the chapters.
In essence, the book Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener demonstrated the importance of setting both short- and long-term goals.
Additionally, it is clear that they placed a strong focus on using time wisely and not wasting it on useless activities.
It can also be seen that you should be careful who you associate with, for example, if you associate with lazy individuals, you will eventually become lazy yourself.
Finally, always strive to learn new things; it is never too late to do so.


My eyes were opened to several shortcomings that I possess after reading the book.
I waste a lot of time on things that aren't really necessary, like watching hours of YouTube.
If I could cut down on that time and use the extra time for something more useful, I know my life would be better.
Daily knowledge increase is a wonderful habit to form because it will increase my understanding and make me more versatile.
